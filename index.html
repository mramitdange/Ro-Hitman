Rohit Sharma

Early Life

Rohit Sharma was born on April 30, 1987, in Bansod, Nagpur, Maharashtra. He was raised by his grandparents and uncles in Borivali because of his father's low income. Rohit excelled in cricket from a young age and joined a cricket camp with his uncle's financial support.

Domestic Career

Rohit Sharma began his domestic career playing for Mumbai in the Ranji Trophy. His impressive performances in domestic cricket earned him a place in the national team.

International Career

Rohit made his ODI debut for India on June 23, 2007, against Ireland. He made his T20I debut on September 19, 2007, against England, and his Test debut on November 6, 2013, against the West Indies.

ODI Records

  • Highest individual score in an ODI: 264 runs against Sri Lanka on November 13, 2014.
  • Only player to score three double centuries in ODIs.
  • Most sixes in an ODI inning: 16 against Australia on November 2, 2013.
  • Fastest player to score 7,000 ODI runs as an opener.
  • Total ODI centuries: 30 (as of 2023).
  • Total ODI double centuries: 3.

T20I Records

  • Leading run-scorer in T20 internationals.
  • First player to score four centuries in T20Is.
  • Fastest T20I century by an Indian (35 balls).
  • Total T20I sixes: 182 (as of 2023).

Test Records

  • Highest score by an Indian opener in Tests outside Asia: 212 runs against South Africa.
  • First player to score twin centuries in his first Test as an opener.
  • Total Test centuries: 8 (as of 2023).

Achievements

  • ICC ODI Cricketer of the Year: 2019
  • Arjuna Award: 2015
  • Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna: 2020
  • Only player to score five centuries in a single World Cup: 2019
  • Fastest Indian to 2000 runs in T20Is

IPL Career

Rohit Sharma is the captain of the Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Under his leadership, Mumbai Indians have won the IPL title five times (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, and 2020). Rohit is one of the most successful captains in IPL history.

  • Total IPL runs: 5,878 (as of 2023).
  • Total IPL centuries: 1.
  • Total IPL half-centuries: 40.
  • Total IPL sixes: 213 (as of 2023).

Personal Life

Rohit Sharma married Ritika Sajdeh in December 2015. The couple has a daughter named Samaira, born in 2018. Outside of cricket, Rohit is known for his philanthropic work, especially in wildlife conservation.
